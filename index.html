Our Story

Meet Aditi Shukla, the founder of Apothecary Candles. What started as a simple hobby quickly transformed into a passionate craft. Each candle is handcrafted with love, using only the finest natural ingredients and essential oils.

"I believe that a good candle can transform not just a room, but your entire mood. Every scent I create is inspired by moments, memories, and the desire to bring peace and joy into people's homes."

From our small workshop to your home, each candle represents our commitment to quality, sustainability, and the art of traditional candle-making.
